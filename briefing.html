Executive Briefing Playbook

The 5-stage conversion blueprint for the $22,000 LMI Diagnostic.

Objective

Confirm the prospect meets minimum criteria before any time is invested on either side.

Owner

Nikki (or delegate). Review within 24 hours.

✓ Advance to Stage 2 when

Decision-making authority confirmed · Org 200+ employees · Investment readiness: Yes or Possibly.

✗ Route to Nurture when

No authority · Org under 50 · "Not at this time" → Send to UNMASK Report.

Research (15 min)

  • Review application responses in full
  • Research org on LinkedIn (size, recent exits)
  • Identify industry-specific structural strain patterns

Emphasis Selection

If CHRO: Lead with retention costs.
If CEO: Lead with misdiagnosis & AI pressure.

Closing Pivot Script
"Based on what you've seen today, the question is no longer whether this is happening in your organization — it's where. The LMI Assessment gives us that map. It's the targeted diagnostic that shows us exactly which structural layers are carrying the heaviest load. That is our precise next step."
Live Question: AI Pressure

"What AI tools has your organization deployed... and what structural changes were made to support them?"

Live Question: Financial Anchor

"How many manager-level exits have you had? Let's put a number on what that has cost you."

Stages 4 & 5 follow standard closing and onboarding protocols.
